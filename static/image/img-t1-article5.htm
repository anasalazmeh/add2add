About WOWBO PACKAGING SOLUTIONS

Information written by the company

More than One-Stop Professional Packaging Solutions Provider. Since 2014 we've been serving our customers from all over the world. We manufacture only premium custom packaging from Shanghai and offer shipment globally. We are unlike most of the packaging suppliers, we care the most about the quality than quantity. Each and every packaging item we make sure it is perfectly made in all aspects such as material-wise, production process, special techniques, or color printing accuracy. We understand the value of how packaging is a silent marketing tool. We’re verified by UKAS, BRCGS, and SGS Global Standard for Packaging and Packaging Materials recognized by the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI). We are specialized in Custom High-Quality Food Packaging and Gift Box Packaging. We do not focus on quantity but only quality. For food packaging, we pay most attention to the materials and their safety, durability, and better quality than our competitors. We see through the problems and try our best to come up with a solution for our customers. For e.g. our paper cups are our signature food packaging as our paper cups are simply one of a kind, with No Smell, No Leakage, or Softening. Most of the paper cups in the market contain a bad paper odor and are easy to be soft and shrink. Therefore, most of our customers who faced those situations before will find us as their problem solvers.
